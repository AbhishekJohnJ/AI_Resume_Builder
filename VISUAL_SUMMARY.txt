🎯 WHAT IT DOES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Input:  Resume PDF or Text
    ↓
  Processing:
    • Extract text from PDF
    • Extract 8 numeric features
    • Generate AI embedding (384-dim)
    • Combine features (392 total)
    • Predict score (0-100)
    • Predict level (4 categories)
    • Generate feedback
    ↓
  Output: Complete Analysis
    • Resume Score (0-100)
    • Resume Level (Excellent/Good/Average/Needs Improvement)
    • 8 Extracted Features
    • 5 Weak Areas
    • 4 Suggestions
    • 4 Recommended Tasks
